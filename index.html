About

SOC Team Lead at TAV Technologies with 5+ years of experience in cybersecurity operations, specializing in aviation infrastructure protection and threat intelligence.

My work focuses on building and managing security operations centers for critical infrastructure, with particular emphasis on aviation-specific threats and compliance requirements. I develop automated threat intelligence platforms, detection engineering frameworks, and incident response playbooks tailored to the unique challenges of aviation security.

Throughout my career, I've worked with SIEM platforms (QRadar, Splunk), EDR/XDR solutions (Microsoft Defender, Cortex), SOAR orchestration, and built custom security automation tools using Python and various threat intelligence APIs.

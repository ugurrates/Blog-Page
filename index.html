About

Security Architect and SOC Team Lead at TAV Technologies with 5+ years of experience in cybersecurity operations, specializing in aviation infrastructure protection, security automation, and enterprise security architecture.

My work focuses on designing and building security operations with an architectural mindset, applying enterprise architecture frameworks such as TOGAF, NAF (NATO Architecture Framework), and DOTMLPFI to security operations and cyber threat intelligence programs. I develop automated threat intelligence platforms, detection engineering frameworks, and incident response playbooks for critical infrastructure environments.

Throughout my career, I've worked with SIEM platforms (QRadar, Splunk), EDR/XDR solutions (Microsoft Defender, Cortex), SOAR orchestration, and built custom security automation tools using Python and various threat intelligence APIs. I also develop open-source blue team tooling including LLM-powered SOC assistants and GRC compliance platforms.
